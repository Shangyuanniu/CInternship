Shenzhen Speed

37 years ago, Baoan was a small fishing village with approximately 70 thousand population. In 1979, the Chinese government decided to develop it into one of the first Special Economic Zones in China, which led to the birth of Shenzhen, a city of ongoing innovation today. People across the country came to build this city into the most diversified and free environment, where most of the greatest businesses in China were born. In 2015, Shenzhen, with 20 Million population, was ranked the most innovative and fastest growing city in China, ahead of Beijing and Shanghai.
