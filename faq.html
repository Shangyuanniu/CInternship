Frequently Asked Questions

Is this program open to applicants of all nationalities?

This program is open to applicants of all nationalities. However, visa application requirements may vary for applicants from some countries.

Where does the program take place?

Participating companies are located in Shenzhen, which has the most developed technology, logistics, and finance industry in China. You might find yourself working amid the towering skyscrapers of downtown Shenzhen or within the beautiful technology parks near Qianhai Free Trade Area.

When does the program take place?

The program will generally take place among July and August. The exact time is negotiable and can be adjusted to fit your schedule. You will receive your exact time together with your placement result within one week after we receive your payment.

When is the deadline for an application?

We normally like to allow at least 3 weeks prior to your program start date to organize your internship placement. In addition, places in some sectors can be limited, therefore we recommend that you apply as early as possible to allow us to find the most suitable company for you.

What qualifications do I need to apply?

All applicants must have completed or be currently enrolled in a degree program. We are also looking for you to exhibit a relatively strong academic record, and a strong interest in Chinese culture and business opportunities. Fluent English is essential; if you speak some Chinese and would like to practice your language skills that would be better and you will have plenty of opportunities to practice your language skill, both with your Chinese colleagues and your one to one peer advisors.

Do I need to speak Chinese?

Chinese language skills are not required. Some of your Chinese colleagues will be able to speak English. We ensure that you have an English-speaking advisor who can act as your guide and provide structure and support throughout your work experience.

Where will I live?

There is an optional accommodation package which can be included in your final payments. If you choose to do so, you will be placed in modern, comfortable, serviced hotels in Shenzhen. Yet you are always encourage to find the best place to live for yourselves.

Which company will I work for?

We arranges internships for both multinational firms and Chinese companies in many industry sectors. We match you with a company according to your own intention and your previous professional experience. We continually work work with our partner companies to ensure that they provide meaningful and valuable internships for you.

About Us

CIntern Program offers talented students currently studying in colleges and graduate students a chance to work inside innovative Chinese startups, logistic firms, and financial institutions. Through the programs students may get to know the brilliant people, magnificent culture, and amazing cuisine of real China. They can also gain first handed entrepreneurial experience and greatly improve their language skills working with brilliant Chinese peers and one to one local advisors.
