About Cinternship

CIntern Program offers talented students currently studying in colleges and graduate students a chance to work inside innovative Chinese startups, logistic firms, and financial institutions. Through the programs students may get to know the brilliant people, magnificent culture, and amazing cuisine of real China. They can also gain first handed entrepreneurial experience and greatly improve their language skills working with brilliant Chinese peers and one to one local advisors. It's also a great opportunity for them to understand Chinese working ethic, Chinese business environment and magnificent Chinese culture.

About Shenzhen

Shenzhen, situated immediately north of Hong Kong, is one of the most dynamic metropolises with China’s most developed technology, financial, and logistics industry. Its rich resources will extremely benefit the internship experience. Different from most other teams that charge for Chinese lessons, during your stay in Shenzhen, C-intern will assign you your own Chinese advisor that will provide you free city guide and Chinese language tutoring. Shenzhen is one of the most developed cites in China, with its beautiful environment, frienly citizens. It continues to attract visitors from all over the world.
